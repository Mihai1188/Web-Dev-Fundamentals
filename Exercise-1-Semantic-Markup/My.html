Chocolate Chip Cookies

Delicious homemade cookies with gooey chocolate chips!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

If you don't have eggs, you can use a suitable egg substitute like applesauce, mashed bananas, or yogurt. Use 1/4 cup of the substitute for each egg in the recipe.

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 190°C.
  2. In a small bowl, mix the flour and baking soda.
  3. Cream together the butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla extract.
  4. Add eggs one at a time.
  5. Gradually mix in the flour mixture.
  6. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  7. Drop dough onto baking sheets.
  8. Bake for 8–10 minutes.
  9. Allow cookies to cool before serving.
